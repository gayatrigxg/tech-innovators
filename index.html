Who Are We?

The Tech Innovators Club at MIT-WPU is a student-led organization focused on web and app development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. We meet regularly to collaborate on projects, enhance technical skills, and stay updated with industry trends through workshops, lectures,
and competitions. Our goal is to empower students to create innovative solutions for tomorrow’s tech challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading platform for students to explore, innovate, and excel in the ever-evolving tech landscape, fostering a community that drives technological progress and empowers individuals to make a meaningful impact.
